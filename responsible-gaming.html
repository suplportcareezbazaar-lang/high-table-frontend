Responsible Use

High Table is committed to promoting a safe, balanced, and responsible digital experience. Our platform is designed for entertainment purposes only and should be used mindfully.

Please ensure that your participation on High Table remains within personal, financial, and time limits that are comfortable for you.

Guidelines for Responsible Use

  • Use the platform only for leisure and entertainment
  • Set personal limits on time and spending
  • Take regular breaks during usage
  • Avoid impulsive or emotional decisions
  • Never use essential or borrowed funds

Know When to Pause

If you feel that your activity is becoming difficult to manage, it is important to pause and reassess your usage. Taking a break can help restore balance and clarity.

High Table supports informed and responsible decision-making at all times. Assistance options are available for users who wish to limit or suspend their access.
