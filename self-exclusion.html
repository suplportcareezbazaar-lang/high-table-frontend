Self Exclusion

High Table provides users with the option to temporarily or permanently restrict access to their account if they feel the need to take a break.

What Is Self Exclusion?

Self exclusion allows you to voluntarily pause your access for a defined period or indefinitely.

When to Consider It

  • Difficulty managing usage time
  • Loss of enjoyment
  • Personal or financial stress
To request self exclusion, please contact our support team. Once activated, access cannot be restored until the exclusion period ends.
